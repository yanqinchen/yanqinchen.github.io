Biography

I am now a 4th year PhD candidate in both Broadband Network & Digital Media Lab and Shenzhen Key Lab of Broadband Network and Multimedia at Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Xin Jin.

My current research interests include: Light Field Photography and Microscopy, and Biomedical Imaging.
